Rules (short):
  • Standard chess moves — validated by chess.js.
  • Castling, en-passant, promotion are supported (promotion auto-queen).
  • To play online: Create a room, share Room ID. First joiner becomes White, second becomes Black. Extra joiners observe.
⚠️ Replace firebaseConfig in app.js with your project's config. For testing you can allow open Realtime DB rules but secure them before production.
